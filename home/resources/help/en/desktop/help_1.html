Operation Steps
1.Make selection by using the direction keys of TVpad
   remote, and pressto enter the relevant functions.
2.After entering any functions, presson the remote
   to return the main menu.

What’s on the TV interface
1.The functions on the left from top to bottom are App
   Manager, My TVpad and Settings.
2.The shortcut of app list on the right shows the apps
   you have installed.
3.It shows the status area of TV on the lower right-hand
  corner (the sequencing is in no particular order): Wireless
  Network (Wi-Fi), Wired Network, USB External Storage
  Disk, TF Card, and the login status.
