Operation Steps
1.Enter factory default page, use the direction keys to select
   “OK”, and then press.
2.Wait for TVpad to restart after the system prompts
  “Restore Succeeded”.
3.Restore will be completed after restart.

Note
All the settings will restore factory values after you choose
“Factory Default”, but installed apps would not be unloaded.
After the restore succeeds, the system would automatically
restart.
