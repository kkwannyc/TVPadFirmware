Operation Steps
1.Use the left and right arrow keys to select successively the
   mode and system of the video output, and select “OK”,
   then press.( Please read “Instructions” of your
   display device before setting mode and system.)
2.Wait for TVpad to restart after the system prompts “Video
   settings succeeded.”
3.Video settings will succeed after restart.

Description
HDMI and AV (composite videos) output are available for
video output, and factory default is HDMI output.
System: System PAL and system NTSC are two categories
of color TV system, and the distinction lies in the difference
of color coding, decoding mode of the program and
the field scanning frequency.
·System NTSC: Mainly used in the United States, Canada,
                     Japan, Korea, Taiwan (China), etc.;
