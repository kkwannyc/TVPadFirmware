Operation Steps
Make selection with the direction keys of TVpad remote, and
pressto enter the relevant functions.

Functions
Recharge, Recharge Records, Messages and Activate Warranty.

Recharge
1.Select “Recharge” and pressto enter.
2.Select Card No. and Password with the direction keys
  of TVpad remote, and pressto enter the soft keyboard.
  When completed, please press to exit the soft keyboard.
（Remarks: Supporting input method of multi-language, press
   <Input Method> button on the TVpad remote to switch.）
3.When all configuration data completed, select “OK” with the
   direction keys of TVpad remote and pressbutton. when
   the system prompt “you have recharged successfully !” ,
   you can pressto exit.
